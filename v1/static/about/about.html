Yared Torres

Local musician of the puertorrican rock, jazz, and punk scene. One of the developers of EscenApp and currently part of Holberton Schools Cohort 15. Currently an advocate for the islands artistic scene while entering the world of front-end development. Having worked in the local music scene for over 10 years he brings his experience to the tech side of things in order to give insight into areas where tech can help an often underrepresented sector of the islands artistic scene.
