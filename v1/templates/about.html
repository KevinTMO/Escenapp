What is EscenApp?

EscenApp is a brand new event platform that allows for local artists to create an artist-based database for events. Often times only big name events are promoted and local talent is left to fend for themselves. Having such a large artistic community from musicians and artists, to painters and actors, we felt  that the gap is never really bridged. Artists often depend on other social media apps to reach the same goal, and the apps aren't particularly for them. Although there are a wide variety of music apps, the other artistic areas sometimes don't receive the same amount of attention.

That is where EscenApp comes in. EscenApp's main goal is to centralize and help artists do one thing, share their events. One place, one mission. No more jumping through apps or endless scrolling hoping to come across something worthwhile. Whether you are from the area or not, you'll be able to access this artist-created database and find an event to your tastes. Be it a concert or an art expo, near or far, you'll be able to find it quickly and be on your way.  We've hope that artists will use our app to their advantage and that users find it easy to find events and keep up with their preferred artists.
